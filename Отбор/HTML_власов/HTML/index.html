Transgummanizm. What it is?

Transhumanism - a rational , based on the understanding of the achievements and prospects of science, a worldview that recognizes the possibility and desirability of a fundamental change in the position of using advanced technology to eliminate suffering , aging and death , and significantly enhance the physical, mental and psychological abilities of a person .

Bioengineering now

is the application of concepts and methods of biology (and secondarily of physics, chemistry, mathematics, and computer science) to solve real-world problems related to SSBS life sciences or the application thereof, using engineering's own analytical and synthetic methodologies and also its traditional sensitivity to the cost and practicality of the solution(s) arrived at. In this context, while traditional engineering applies physical and mathematical sciences to analyze, design and manufacture inanimate tools, structures and processes, biological engineering uses primarily the rapidly developing body of knowledge known as molecular biology to study and advance applications of organisms and to create biotechnology.
